# MotoTrackr Privacy Policy

**Related:** [Terms of Use](https://mototrackrdev.github.io/privacy-policy/terms-of-use.html)

**Effective Date:** April 26, 2026

## Overview

MotoTrackr is a companion app for motorcycle track day enthusiasts. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

**MotoTrackr does NOT collect any personal data.**

All information you enter into the app — including:
- Your display name
- Profile information
- Motorcycle details
- Track sessions and lap times
- Track maps and locations

...is stored **locally on your device only** using Android/iOS native storage (SharedPreferences).

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identification information
- Location data (GPS)
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any data on external servers
- Advertising identifiers

## Third-Party Services

MotoTrackr does not use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage services
- Social media integrations

## Data Security

Since all data remains on your device:
- We do not operate any servers that could be breached
- We do not transmit data over networks
- Your data is as secure as your device

If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be removed.

## Children's Privacy

MotoTrackr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's support channels or the GitHub repository.
